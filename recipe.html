Grandma's Classic Apple Pie

A warm and comforting dessert with a flaky crust

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, chilled
  • 5 large apples, peeled and sliced
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 egg, beaten for egg wash

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Mix flour and salt in a large bowl, then cut in butter until the texture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Add cold water a tablespoon at a time and form the dough into two discs.
  4. Roll out one disc and fit it into a 9-inch pie dish.
  5. Combine apple slices, sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a bowl.
  6. Pour the apple filling into the prepared crust and arrange evenly.
  7. Roll out the second disc, place it over the filling, and seal the edges.
  8. Brush the top crust with beaten egg and cut a few vents for steam.
  9. Bake for 45-50 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
  10. Let the pie cool for at least 20 minutes before slicing and serving.
